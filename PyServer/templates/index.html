ABOUT US

Hi, we're Brindr. If you're a shrimp looking for deep and meaningful connections, you've come to the right place.

While there are always other fish in the sea, we at Brindr know that it can be nearly shrimpossible to find the krill for you. No more feeling like a prawn in your relationships-- we've made it our mission to find you the perfect partner, pincers and all.

In our increasingly shellfish world, seeking out your own lover can land you in hot water. That's why Brindr strives to provide an oasis of honest, genuine love. Whether you're a giant tiger prawn on the prowl in the Indian Ocean or a sakura shrimp from Suruga Bay, our site can connect you with caring crustaceans in your colony.

Our small team is committed to your satisfaction.
